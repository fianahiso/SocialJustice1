STANDARDIZED TESTING

Why students shouldnt live and die by the numbers on their test

In every school there are bright, smart, and talented students. However, when the time comes to place students in advanced classes, some of these students are placed in the common core classes. They aren't given the chance to prosper and reach their full potential. The reason behind this is because the placement of advanced classes relies soley on test. Teachers aren't given the oppertunity or chance to decide or observe the true potential of bright students. The advanced placement relies on test scores solely. For some, this may not be of any concern. But for others who are truley talented but don't do well on these tests, the same cannot be said. They are held back by being put in a class in which will not challenge or give them the chance to prosper. It's important that teachers recognize the potential of students, and that this is taken into account when placing kids in classes. Test scores soley are not the only factor or element that should be taken into account when looking for talented students. Without being challenged, how are student's supposed to do better? How are they supposed to learn and become better? Is it fair that bright students are being deprived from the proper learning that they need only because their test scores don't quite make the cut? There are so many other ways to decide who is a bright student with real potential. Not all bright students do well when it comes to tests, but this in no way means that they are not talented.

Talent comes in many different forms, and you can't judge a student's value just by looking at the numbers on a test..
